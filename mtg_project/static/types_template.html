A card type is a characteristic that each Magic: the Gathering card has. Non-traditional Magic cards and tokens also have card types. Each card type has its own rules for how objects with that type are played, cast, or otherwise handled during gameplay.

A Subtype is a characteristic of a card. Every card has a card type and may have one or more subtypes.

Example: "Basic Land -- Mountain" means the card is a land with the subtype Mountain. "Creature -- Goblin Wizard" means the card is a creature with the subtypes Goblin and Wizard. "Artifact -- Equipment" means the card is an artifact with the subtype Equipment.
